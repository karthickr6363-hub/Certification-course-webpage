Biography

John Smith is a seasoned AWS Solutions Architect with over a decade of experience in cloud computing and infrastructure design. He has worked with Fortune 500 companies to design and implement scalable, secure, and cost-effective cloud solutions on AWS.

John's expertise spans across various AWS services including EC2, S3, Lambda, CloudFormation, and many more. He is passionate about sharing his knowledge and has helped thousands of students achieve their AWS certifications and advance their careers in cloud computing.

Prior to becoming an instructor, John worked as a Senior Solutions Architect at Amazon Web Services, where he helped enterprise clients migrate to the cloud. He holds multiple AWS certifications and is a regular speaker at cloud computing conferences worldwide.
