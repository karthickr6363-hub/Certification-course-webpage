   - Student Testimonials
   - Newsletter Signup
   - Footer with quick links

   - Certification Hero Banner
   - Stats (Students Trained, Certificates Issued)

   - Related courses
   - Downloadable syllabus PDF

5. **Instructor Pages**
   - **Instructor Listing** (`instructors.html`) - Grid view with filters
   - **Instructor Profile** (`instructor-profile.html`) - Detailed profile with bio, courses, and reviews

6. **Live Classes/Webinars** (`live-classes.html`)
   - Upcoming live sessions with join/register buttons
   - Recorded webinars archive

7. **Certification Exam Module** (`certification-exam.html`)
   - Exam categories
   - Mock test registration
   - Requirements & validity information
   - Certificate sample preview
   - How certification works (step-by-step process)

8. **Pricing Page** (`pricing.html`)
   - Free, Standard, and Premium plans
   - Feature comparison
   - Billing FAQ

9. **Blog Module**
   - **Blog Listing** (`blog.html`) - Category filters, pagination
   - **Blog Details** (`blog-details.html`) - Full article with author bio and related posts

11. **Authentication Pages**

    - **Register** (`register.html`) - Split-screen design
    - **Forgot Password** (`forgot-password.html`) - Simple centered form

12. **Admin Dashboard** (`admin-dashboard.html`)
    - Overview dashboard with stats
    - Sidebar navigation
    - Manage Courses, Users, Instructors
    - Enrollments tracking
    - Earnings & Analytics
    - Certificates issued
    - Messages/Support tickets
    - Settings

13. **Utility Pages**
    - **404 Page** (`404.html`) - Custom error page
    - **Coming Soon** (`coming-soon.html`) - Countdown timer
    - **Terms & Conditions** (`terms.html`)
    - **Privacy Policy** (`privacy.html`)
    - **Maintenance Mode** (`maintenance.html`)
